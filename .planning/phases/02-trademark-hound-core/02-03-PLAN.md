    7. Observe informational exclusion (Wikipedia, news URLs should be logged as excluded)
    8. Observe per-lead investigation and scoring tables with evidence citations

    - Every factor has: score, weight, evidence (non-empty string)
    - risk_tier is "High" or "Medium" only (no "Low" entries)
    - total_score matches risk_tier (>=15 = High, 10-14 = Medium)

    **Spot-check scoring quality:**

    - Compare the evidence citations in the JSON to what you see on the page
    - Evidence should cite real content from the page, not generic statements
